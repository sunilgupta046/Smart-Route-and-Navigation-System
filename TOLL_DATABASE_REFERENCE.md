## Factors Affecting Actual Toll

Your calculated toll may vary due to:

1. **Vehicle Classification**:
   - Car (2-axle): Base rate (₹10)
   - SUV (heavy 2-axle): +20% (₹20)
   - Truck (3+ axle): +50-100% (₹75-100)
   - Bus (2-axle): +30% (₹35)

2. **Payment Method**:
   - **FASTag** (RFID): -12.5% discount
   - **Cash**: Full price (100%)
   - **Online**: -5% discount

3. **Time-based Discounts**:
   - Night travel (10 PM - 5 AM): -10% (some operators)
   - Off-peak: Sometimes -5%

4. **Seasonal**: Some operators have peak/off-peak
